What Alignment Guide is?

Alignment Guide is a selfhelp tool for you to use in the way you wish: wether you want to focus on your physical or mental health or both, you can do it here. We offer different approaches for different people. Here you can get to know yourself and find the suitable way for you to improve your life.

Our services include:

  • nutrition planning
  • movement guides
  • mental health guides
  • physical health diary

You can choose wether you want to calculate your calories and macros without obsessing about them, or if you want to learn how to eat intuitively but still achieve your health goals - Alignment Guide can help with both approaches.

If your goal is to include movement into your life but you haven't found a way to make it a habit, Alignment Guide can help you with finding physical hobbies curated to your needs, and getting started with them. We also have guides on how to stay consistent. And even if you already have an active lifestyle, here you can get introduced to different hobbies to make movement even more fulfilling.

Alignment Guide's mental health section can be used as a regular diary in whatever way you need. We also have guides on how you can get to know yourself, to get clear on how you as an individual can improve your life.

Lastly, you can use Alignment Guide for logging in physical symptoms. You can log in your energy levels, symptoms when you experience pain or sickness, and you can also track your menstrual cycle. This is in case you want to monitor your physical health to get more spesific understanding of your body's needs.
